This is the page for information on the Zig NYC group, an organization dedicated to in-person meetups for people using or curious about Zig. You can sign up to receive invitations above and read about previous meetings below.

Come join us and talk or listen about projects, usage, epiphanies, solutions, or gripes with zig ——. No experience is required, the curious are very welcome.

For now, we like to start with short presentations, and then go around introducing ourselves before we break into groups to work or talk together.
